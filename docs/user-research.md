# User Research Execution Plan

## Goals
- Validate alert frequency preferences and content expectations.
- Understand workflows around tracking NSF/Grants.gov opportunities.
- Measure perceived value of analytics and collaboration features.

## Personas & Recruitment
- **University Research Admins**: coordinate funding pipelines across departments.
- **Early-career Researchers**: PIs responsible for finding funding for labs.
- **Nonprofit Development Leads**: chase grants for program expansion.

Recruitment channels: personal network, LinkedIn groups, Grant Professionals Association forums, NSF listserv replies. Offer $50 gift card incentive per interview.

## Timeline
- Week 1: recruitment emails, screener survey (Typeform) with qualification logic.
- Week 2: 6× discovery interviews (remote via Zoom). Recordings auto-uploaded to shared Drive.
- Week 3: usability sessions on Figma prototype (5 participants). Collect SUS + qualitative notes.
- Week 4: quantitative survey (n≥30) measuring feature prioritization and intent to adopt.

## Interview Guide (Discovery)
1. Role, responsibilities, existing tooling.
2. Recent NSF/grant opportunity tracked: process, tools, challenges.
3. Frequency/timing expectations for alerts.
4. Collaboration and approval workflows inside team.
5. Reactions to proposed feature list (feeds, RAG assistant, bookmarking).
6. Willingness to pay / budget constraints.

## Concept Test Tasks
- Navigate dashboard, identify relevant funding in <60 seconds.
- Adjust alert settings to receive weekly summaries with Slack delivery.
- Ask assistant for eligibility clarification and interpret output confidence.
- Save opportunity, export .ics deadline, and share with colleague.

## Success Metrics
- Interview insights mapped into JTBD statements.
- SUS score ≥70 from usability sessions.
- At least 50% respondents willing to rely on weekly alert digest.
- Top 3 feature priorities validated for MVP backlog.

## Tools & Logistics
- Calendly for scheduling.
- Zoom + Otter.ai for transcription.
- FigJam board for affinity mapping insights.
- Notion database to track participants, session notes, highlights.

## Output Artifacts
- Research report (executive summary + detailed findings).
- Persona updates with evidence tags.
- Opportunity solution tree referencing validated problems.
- Backlog adjustments documented in Linear.
